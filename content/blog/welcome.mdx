At New Plains, we believe that the most meaningful technology is the kind that fits naturally into your day. We are an AI innovation studio grounded in a simple idea: artificial intelligence should be practical, accessible, and human-centered. Rather than chasing hype, we focus on building tools that solve real problems for real people — the kind of work that feels less like science fiction and more like a reliable pair of hands.

Our flagship projects reflect that philosophy. **Herm.Chat** brings conversational AI into customer-facing workflows, helping businesses engage with their audience in a way that feels personal and responsive without burning out their teams. **PillBuddy.AI** applies the same practical thinking to healthcare, giving patients a straightforward way to understand their medications and stay on track with their routines. Both tools were born from the same question we ask ourselves every day: *Where can AI quietly make life a little better?*

The plains have always been a place of honest work, open horizons, and steady growth. That is the spirit we carry into everything we build. We are a small studio, and we like it that way. It keeps us close to the people we serve and quick to adapt when we see a better path forward. Every line of code we ship is measured against a standard that has nothing to do with novelty and everything to do with usefulness.

This blog is where we will share what we are learning along the way — updates on our projects, thoughts on the evolving AI landscape, and the occasional look behind the curtain at how we approach our craft. We are glad you are here, and we hope you will stick around as the story unfolds.
